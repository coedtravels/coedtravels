FAQ

Questions, answered

Does it read my email?

Only when you ask it to sweep, only for booking confirmations, only on your machine through your own Claude account. Nothing is uploaded to us.

Do I need Claude AI?

Yes — Coed Travels runs on Claude (by Anthropic). The browser edition works with a free claude.ai account; the Claude app edition needs a paid plan with Cowork.

Which airlines and hotels does it recognize?

70+ verified senders at launch — every major US carrier, Asian low-cost carriers, Agoda, Booking.com, Klook, Viator, and bank travel portals — growing weekly.

Can it rebuild past trips?

Yes — set the dates and it searches the year before the trip, so you can reconstruct trips you took years ago.
